🚀 Moving Beyond AI Chat Wrappers: Introducing Manomaya — The Cinematic Imagination Engine 🎬

Most AI storytelling feels flat. Characters have no memory, relationships don't evolve, and everyone agrees too easily.

We built **Manomaya** — an AI-powered imagination engine designed to turn raw ideas into persistent, visual alternate realities.

Key Features:
🔮 Procedural 3D Character Studio (Character 3D): Design characters in an interactive 3D viewport (height, face shapes, hair). This visual DNA ensures 100% visual consistency in generated images/videos.

📖 Story Bible Engine: A living database that automatically tracks events, active story threads, character arcs, and world rules across scenes to keep your narrative consistent.

🧠 Semantic Vector Memory: Powered by ChromaDB. Characters don't just read chat history—they recall relevant past memories (secrets, arguments, promises) when emotional context triggers them.

🧬 Character Brain & Hidden Thoughts: Characters have goals, fears, and attachment styles. They generate dual-layer dialogue (what they say vs. what they actually think), bringing deep subtext.

📊 Relationship Canvas: Map and track directional dynamics (e.g. trust, comfort, awkwardness) on a visual graph that updates dynamically based on scene outcomes.

🎬 Cinematic Director Engine: Generates framing, lighting, camera angles, and prompts for consistent storyboard keyframing.

🛠️ The Technical Stack & Architecture:
• **Semantic Memory**: ChromaDB vector database (using text embeddings for similarity retrieval) + MongoDB for persistent state.
• **GenAI Core**: FastAPI backend communicating with Groq's high-speed Llama 3.1 8B API for low-latency dialog and director orchestration.
• **3D viewport**: Three.js & React Three Fiber (R3F) for real-time procedural morphing of face shapes and character height.
• **Frontend**: Next.js 16 (App Router) with Zustand state synchronization.

🎯 Use Cases:
✍️ Novelists & Storytellers: Offload tracking complex relationship webs, branching timelines, and world consistency.
🎬 Filmmakers & Writers: Prototype screenplays, run emotional chemistry tests, and generate storyboard sequences.
🎮 Game Devs: Design persistent worlds with self-directing NPCs that remember player choices.
🧠 Creators: Reconstruct vivid dreams and explore "what-if" branching timelines.
